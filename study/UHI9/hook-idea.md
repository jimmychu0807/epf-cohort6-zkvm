### **🏁 UHI9 Hookathon:** Impermanent Loss & Yield Systems

---

The goal here is to push hook innovation toward yield-protected liquidity systems that shield LPs from impermanent loss while unlocking sustainable, predictable on-chain returns.

**What builders could create:**

Hook Idea: IL Insurance Hooks
Description: Impermanent-loss insurance hooks that offset losses via dynamic rebalancing or premium accrual

Hook Idea: YieldBasis-Style Fixed Income
Description: Fixed-income systems inspired by YieldBasis or Pendle that create predictable, sustainable returns for LPs

Hook Idea: Delta-Neutral Hooks
Description: Hooks that hedge LP exposure using per-block swaps, options, or external hedging protocols

Hook Idea: Fee-Smoothing Hooks
Description: Hooks that automatically reinvest or distribute yield over time, smoothing fee flow for LPs

Hook Idea: Cross-Pool Hedging Routers
Description: Routers that match opposing liquidity positions across correlated pairs for cross-pool hedging
